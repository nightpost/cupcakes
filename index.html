FRESH

CRANBERRY

MUFFINS

By Annie One

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2/3 cup sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup fresh cranberries
  • 2/3 cup 2% reduced-fat milk
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1 teaspoon grated orange rind
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 large egg, lightly beaten
  • Cooking spray

PREPARATION

Lightly spoon flour into dry measuring cups; level with a knife. Combine flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt in a large bowl; stir well with a whisk. Stir in cranberries; make a well in center of mixture. Combine milk, butter, rind, vanilla, and egg; add to flour mixture, stirring just until moist. Spoon batter into 12 muffin cups coated with cooking spray. Bake until muffins spring back when touched lightly in center.
